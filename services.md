## Network Design & Architecture

A well-designed network is the foundation of everything your business does. We design and implement enterprise network solutions that are resilient, scalable, and aligned with your operational needs.

**What we deliver:**

- Campus and branch network design (wired & wireless)
- Data center network architecture (spine-leaf, fabric-based)
- SD-WAN design and deployment for distributed organizations
- Network segmentation and micro-segmentation
- Network assessment, audits, and optimization
- Migration planning and execution

**Technologies we work with:** Cisco, Juniper, Arista, Aruba, Meraki, VeloCloud, Fortinet SD-WAN

## Cloud & Infrastructure

Moving to the cloud — or optimizing what you already have — requires a partner who understands both the technology and the business drivers behind it. We help you build hybrid and multi-cloud environments that are secure, cost-effective, and easy to manage.

**What we deliver:**

- Cloud readiness assessments and migration planning
- AWS, Azure, and GCP architecture and deployment
- Hybrid cloud and multi-cloud design
- Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Ansible, CloudFormation)
- Virtualization and containerization (VMware, Kubernetes, Docker)
- Cost optimization and cloud governance

**Technologies we work with:** AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud, VMware, Terraform, Ansible, Kubernetes

## Cybersecurity

Threats don't sleep, and neither should your defenses. We provide end-to-end security services — from architecture and implementation to monitoring and incident response — so you can focus on your business with confidence.

**What we deliver:**

- Security architecture design and review
- Next-generation firewall deployment and management

- SIEM deployment and 24/7 security monitoring
- Vulnerability assessments and penetration testing
- Incident response planning and execution
- Compliance readiness (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, PCI-DSS)

**Technologies we work with:** Palo Alto Networks, Fortinet, CrowdStrike, Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, Zscaler
